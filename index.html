Teaching Associate & PhD in Finance

Haitong Jiang

University of Bristol  ·  School of Accounting and Finance

I am a Teaching Associate and researcher specialising in quantitative finance. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, derivative pricing, and climate-related financial risk. I completed my PhD at the University of Reading — thesis: Applications of Machine Learning in Finance, supervised by Prof. Emese Lazar and Dr. Miriam Marra.

I combine rigorous quantitative methods with research-informed teaching, helping students connect financial theory with modern technologies and data analysis, including hands-on work with Bloomberg Terminal and LSEG Workspace.

Research interests: option pricing · asset pricing · climate finance · ESG · machine learning · time series forecasting · financial econometrics · FinTech · quantum finance
